Superbowl '26

We showed up and had a bowl!

If you think Interhigh athletics was just about athletics, scoreboards and school pride... well best believe that the Sunnies pulled up as they usually do. Securing second position with a show worthy of the Super Bowl halftime spotlight.

This was after weeks of hard felt sweat and dedication from our spirit filled cheerleaders who were leading with integrity, our coaches and teachers, who were equipped with original ideas that added the perfect touch. Last but most: our spectators, consisting of volunteers from grade 9 - 12 and of course, the minions - the Grade 8 class of 2026 who cheered their hearts out, winning us the best intro award.

Fun was had, memories were created and never to be forgotten for it was indeed a wonderfull day
