Mission & Vision

Building confident, compassionate scholars

The mission of our school is to foster academic, social, and extra-curricular growth through the development of cognitive thinking skills. We provide educational opportunities for all cultural groups in our community and serve its diverse needs by promoting a culture of thinking and learning. We undertake to:

  • Promote cognitive thinking
  • Teach skills for problem solving
  • Develop intrinsic motivation and reflective thinking
  • Provide structures to develop the total person
  • Nurture a spirit of tolerance and honesty
  • Respond to the needs of the marketplace and broader community
  • Uphold values and norms based on respect for the dignity of all life
